# Evaluation Limitations

- Current evaluation cases are small and application-oriented.
- Video files are local and are not committed to Git.
- Human review is required before claiming content-quality differences.
- Automated tests verify metric calculations and workflow contracts, not final video quality.
- LLM outputs can vary by model, prompt, and temperature.
- Chinese ASR remains a major bottleneck for content selection.
- This evaluation is not a public benchmark comparison and does not support claims of SOTA or algorithmic superiority.
